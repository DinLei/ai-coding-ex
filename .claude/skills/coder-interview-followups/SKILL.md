# coder-interview-followups

Use this skill to simulate interviewer follow-ups after a solution has been discussed or implemented.

## Goals
- Predict likely interviewer questions
- Strengthen verbal explanation ability
- Prepare concise, high-quality answers
- Surface weak spots before the real interview

## Rules
- Focus on realistic interview follow-ups
- Prioritize correctness, complexity, tradeoffs, edge cases, and extensions
- Keep answers concise but strong
- Do not wander into unrelated theory

## Output format

### 1. Likely Follow-Up Questions
Provide 5 to 8 likely interviewer questions.

### 2. Strong Answer for Each
For each question, provide:
- a concise answer
- what makes that answer strong
- what mistake candidates often make here

### 3. Harder Variant
Suggest one harder twist or requirement change and explain how the current approach would need to adapt.

## Style
- Simulate real interview pressure
- Optimize for spoken explanation, not essay writing
